What is Home Grown?

Home Grown is a closed loop Aquaponic System that allows people to grow healthy, nutricious food in an urban environment. It combines modern IoT technologies with century old farming techniques to create a miniture farm without the need for a garden.

The video below shows a small scale prototype built in my Garden. The aim of this project was to prove that this was technicaly feasible. Some good news - it is! Please get in touch if you want to come visit!

What is Aquaponics?

Aquaponics is a combination of Aquaculture and Hydroponics. Aquaculture is the farming of aquatic organisms and Hydroponics is the growing of plants using mineral nutrient solutions

How does Aquaponics work?

Remember Biology GCSE's? Well the process at work is the Nitrogen cycle. It's really quite a simple process but one that has had millenia to be perfected. The joy of Aquaponics is the ability to harness this pefection!

  1. The fish create waste from feeding
  2. The waste is essentially ammonia
  3. Bacteria convert the ammonia into nitrates and nitrites
  4. The plants absorb the nitrates out of the system
  5. The plants clean the water for the fish
